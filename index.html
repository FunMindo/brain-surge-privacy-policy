How We Use Information

  • To save your game progress, scores, coins, and preferences locally on your device
  • To deliver advertisements through Google AdMob and keep the game free to play
  • To measure ad performance and manage ad frequency (handled entirely by AdMob)
  • To comply with applicable Indian law

FunMindo does not sell, rent, trade, or share your information with any third party for marketing purposes. We do not use your data for profiling or tracking outside of what is described in this policy.

3

Local Data Storage

Brain Surge stores all game data exclusively on your device. This data never leaves your device and is not transmitted to FunMindo or any third party.

  • What is stored locally: quiz scores, coin balance, daily challenge completion, lifeline availability, category progress, and sound settings
  • When is it deleted: automatically when you clear app data in your device settings, or when you uninstall the Game
  • Can FunMindo access it: No — FunMindo operates no server or backend and has no mechanism to receive or view data stored on your device

You can opt out of personalised ads at any time:

  • Android: Settings → Google → Ads → "Opt out of Ads Personalisation"
  • Reset Advertising ID: Settings → Google → Ads → "Reset advertising ID"

Opting out means you will still see ads, but they will not be personalised. Game functionality is not affected.

5

Google Play Data Safety

In accordance with Google Play's Data Safety requirements, Brain Surge uses Google AdMob, which may collect the following types of data for advertising and analytics purposes:

  • Device or other identifiers — such as the Android Advertising ID, used to serve and measure ads
  • App interactions — including information about how ads are viewed and interacted with
  • Diagnostics — crash logs and performance data that may be collected by AdMob or Google Play Services

This data is collected and processed by Google, not by FunMindo, and is governed by Google's Privacy Policy: policies.google.com/privacy

FunMindo does not collect, share, or sell any data for advertising purposes independently. All ad-related data collection is handled entirely by Google AdMob.

When completing the Google Play Console Data Safety form, data collection and sharing should be declared under Google AdMob's SDK data practices, as FunMindo itself does not collect user data directly.

Legal Basis for Processing

Under India's Digital Personal Data Protection Act, 2023 (DPDPA), FunMindo processes data on the following bases:

  • Consent — By downloading and playing Brain Surge, you consent to Google AdMob collecting device identifiers and Advertising IDs for ad delivery. You may withdraw this at any time by uninstalling the Game or opting out of personalised ads in device settings (see Section 4).
  • Legitimate use — Local storage of game progress on your own device is a legitimate operational necessity for the Game to function.
  • Legal obligation — We may retain information as required by the IT Act, 2000 and DPDPA 2023.

Children's Privacy

Data Security

Brain Surge stores all game data locally on your device and does not transmit it to any FunMindo server. The security of locally stored game data depends on the security of your own device.

The only external data handling occurs through Google AdMob and Google Play Services, both of which maintain their own industry-standard security practices. FunMindo does not intentionally collect personal information and therefore takes reasonable steps to minimise any data exposure through its third-party integrations.

Your Rights
